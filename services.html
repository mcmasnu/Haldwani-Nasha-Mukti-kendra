What We Offer

Comprehensive Healing,
Every Step of the Way

Our programmes address every dimension of recovery — physical, emotional, psychological and spiritual — guided by clinical expertise and human compassion.

💊

Drug De-Addiction

Medically supervised detox and comprehensive rehabilitation for all forms of substance dependence — from prescription drugs to hard narcotics.

Key Benefits
Safe medical detoxification under 24/7 clinical supervision
Individualised rehabilitation plan tailored to substance and severity
Relapse prevention training and coping strategies
Long-term sobriety maintenance with structured aftercare
Who It Helps
Individuals dependent on opioids, cannabis, stimulants or prescription medications
Those who have relapsed and need structured support to break the cycle
Families seeking professional intervention for a loved one

🍃

Alcohol De-Addiction

Evidence-based treatment for alcohol use disorder, combining medical management with deep psychological healing for sustainable sobriety.

Key Benefits
Medically managed alcohol withdrawal to prevent complications
Cognitive Behavioural Therapy to address underlying triggers
Group therapy and peer support to reduce isolation
Family education to rebuild trust and support systems
Who It Helps
Individuals with mild to severe alcohol dependency
Those whose careers, relationships or health are affected by drinking
People seeking a fresh, sober and purposeful life
Enquire Now →

Family Counselling

Rebuilding the bonds that addiction fractures — through guided therapy for the entire family unit, fostering understanding, trust and healing together.

Key Benefits
Improved communication and conflict resolution within the family
Education for family members on addiction as a medical condition
Healing trauma and co-dependency patterns
Strategies to create a supportive home environment post-rehab
Who It Helps
Spouses, parents, children and siblings of individuals in recovery
Families dealing with breakdown in communication or trust
Anyone experiencing emotional distress due to a loved one's addiction
Enquire Now →
🧠

Behavioural Therapy

Clinical psychological therapies including CBT, DBT and motivational interviewing to transform thinking patterns and build lasting behavioural change.

Key Benefits
Identifies and rewires negative thought patterns driving addictive behaviour
Develops emotion regulation, distress tolerance and mindfulness skills
One-on-one and group therapy formats available
Builds resilience against peer pressure and environmental triggers
Who It Helps
Individuals with addiction, anxiety, anger issues or compulsive behaviours
Those who have struggled with traditional talk therapy alone
Anyone seeking deeper self-understanding and lasting change
Enquire Now →
🌸

Mental Wellness Support

Dedicated psychiatric and psychological care for anxiety, depression, PTSD and co-occurring mental health conditions that often underlie addiction.

Key Benefits
Comprehensive psychiatric evaluation and diagnosis
Medication management when clinically indicated
Evidence-based psychotherapy tailored to each individual
Holistic wellness activities including yoga, breathwork and nature therapy
Who It Helps
Individuals with depression, anxiety, PTSD or bipolar disorder
Those with dual diagnosis — addiction alongside a mental health condition
Anyone feeling emotionally overwhelmed, lost or disconnected from life
Enquire Now →
✨

Detox Programs

A safe, medically guided first step to clear substances from the body — carried out with compassion, medical precision and continuous monitoring.

Key Benefits
Clinically monitored withdrawal management to ensure safety and comfort
Nutritional support and physical recovery during detox
Seamless transition into the full rehabilitation programme
Psychological support throughout the detox process
Who It Helps
Individuals beginning their recovery journey for the first time
Those returning to rehabilitation after a relapse
Anyone requiring medically safe substance clearance before therapy begins
