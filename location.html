Getting Here

How to Reach HLH Haldwani

Haldwani is well-connected and accessible from across North India. We also assist with travel arrangements on request.

✈️ By Air

Pantnagar Airport (PGH) is approximately 30 km from Haldwani — the nearest airport. Dehradun Jolly Grant Airport is about 150 km and connects to major cities including Delhi, Mumbai and Bangalore.

🚂 By Train

Haldwani Railway Station (HDW) is in the heart of the city. Well-connected via trains from Delhi (Kathgodam Express), Lucknow, Agra and other UP/Uttarakhand cities.

🚌 By Road

Haldwani is 280 km from Delhi (~5.5 hours), easily reachable via NH-9. Regular buses operate from Delhi ISBT, Lucknow and Bareilly. Auto, cab and e-rickshaw services are available locally.

Need Help Arranging Travel?

We understand that travelling for treatment can be overwhelming. Call or WhatsApp us — we will personally guide you or your family member, step by step.
