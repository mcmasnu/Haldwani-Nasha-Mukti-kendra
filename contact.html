Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if someone needs rehab?

If substance use is affecting daily life, relationships or health — it's time to speak to a professional. Call us and we will guide you honestly.

Is the process confidential?

Absolutely. Every enquiry, admission and treatment at HLH is completely confidential. We take privacy very seriously.

How long does rehabilitation take?

Duration varies by individual need — typically 30 to 90 days for residential programmes. A counsellor will advise after an initial assessment.

Can families visit during treatment?

Yes, family involvement is central to our approach. Supervised family visits are scheduled as part of the treatment plan.

What if someone refuses to come?

We offer family coaching and intervention guidance. Often, family support and professional guidance can help motivate a loved one to seek help.

Do you provide aftercare support?

Yes. Our structured aftercare programme includes follow-up counselling, support groups and relapse prevention plans for sustained recovery.
